## 5. Automation: The "Access Manager"
This script ensures only paying users stay in the channel.

*   **Payment Webhook:** `POST /api/v1/payments/webhook`
    *   Verifies Razorpay Signature.
    *   Finds/Creates `SignalUser` in DB.
    *   Sets `expiry_date = now() + 30 days`.
    *   Sends a private Telegram message with a **Unique One-Time Join Link**.
*   **The Reaper (Daily 12:00 AM):**
    ```python
    def cleanup_expired_members():
        expired_users = db.query(SignalUser).filter(SignalUser.expiry_date < now()).all()
        for user in expired_users:
            telegram_api.kick_chat_member(chat_id=settings.PREMIUM_CHAT_ID, user_id=user.telegram_id)
            user.subscription_status = 'expired'
            db.commit()
    ```

## 19. Undecenary Monetization: AI 'Trade Autopsy' & PnL Analyzer
**Concept:** A diagnostic tool where users upload broker screenshots or PDFs, and the AI "replays" the market to tell them exactly why they made or lost money.

### A. Detailed Implementation Spec (The "Why" Engine)

#### 1. Data Extraction Pipeline (The "Eye")
*   **PDF Parsing:** Use `PyMuPDF` or `pdfplumber` to extract table data from standard Zerodha/Upstox/Dhan contract notes.
*   **Screenshot OCR:** Use `EasyOCR` or `pytesseract`. 
    *   *Logic:* Train a simple coordinate map for each broker (e.g., Zerodha's PnL is always at X,Y coordinate).
    *   *Extracted Fields:* `Symbol`, `Buy_Time`, `Sell_Time`, `Entry_Price`, `Exit_Price`, `Quantity`.

#### 2. Market Replay Engine (The "Time Machine")
*   **Historical Query:** Once the time is extracted (e.g., 10:15 AM), query the `historical_data` table for a window of `-30 mins` to `+30 mins` around the trade.
*   **Indicator Overlay:** Re-run `services/indicators.py` on this window to see the state of RSI, MACD, and Supertrend at the **exact second** the user clicked "Buy."

#### 3. AI Diagnosis Logic (The "Doctor")
The system compares the user's trade against "Ideal Patterns":
*   **Pattern 1: "The FOMO Trap"**
    *   *Detection:* User bought when RSI > 75 and Price was > 2% away from 20-EMA.
    *   *Diagnosis:* "You chased a vertical move. The trade failed because you bought at the exhaustion point."
*   **Pattern 2: "The Revenge Trade"**
    *   *Detection:* User took 3 losing trades in 10 minutes, and the 4th trade (this one) was with 2x the normal quantity.
    *   *Diagnosis:* "Emotional Over-trading detected. You doubled your size to recover losses, leading to a catastrophic drawdown."
*   **Pattern 3: "The Weak Breakout"**
    *   *Detection:* User bought a breakout, but `volume_profiler` shows volume was < 20-day average.
    *   *Diagnosis:* "Low-confidence breakout. There was no institutional 'Big Money' supporting this move."

#### 4. Visualization (The "X-Ray")
*   **React Frontend:** Display a Candlestick chart using `Recharts`.
*   **Markers:** Use a **Green Arrow** for the user's Entry and a **Red Arrow** for the Exit.
*   **Overlay:** Show the "AI Recommended Entry" vs. "User's Actual Entry" to show the "Slippage of Logic."

### B. Business Value & MRR
*   **Lead Magnet:** "Upload your last 10 losses for free, and we'll tell you your biggest behavioral flaw."
*   **Subscription (₹799/mo):** Includes a "Weekly Performance Audit" PDF sent to their email every Saturday, summarizing their trading psychology.
